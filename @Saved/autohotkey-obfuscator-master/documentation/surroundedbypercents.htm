Obfuscation of Autohotkey Variables in Your Script Surrounded by %'s

This program can replace occurances of variables in your program that start with a % and end with a %. No special action by you is necessary, just follow the normal rules for obfuscation of variables.
 
var1 = %var2%
var3 = %parameter1%
 
gui, add, text, %controlwidth%,
(join`s
bla bla bla....%saythis%
)
